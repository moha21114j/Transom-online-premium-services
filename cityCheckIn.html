Check-in at City Locations

Maraheb's City Check-in service offers convenient locations throughout Muscat where you can complete your check-in process before traveling to the airport. Our fixed offices are strategically located in key areas to serve you better.

Simply visit any of our city locations with your travel documents, and our experienced staff will handle your check-in, seat selection, and baggage processing. This service helps you avoid airport crowds and ensures a smoother travel experience.

  • Multiple convenient city locations
  • Professional check-in assistance
  • Baggage handling and processing
  • Seat selection and boarding pass printing
  • Travel document verification
  • Flight status updates
  • Reduced airport waiting time
